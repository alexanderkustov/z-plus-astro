\-----**About Us**

**Overview**

Zenex Plus (Z+), headquartered in the City of London, offers one-stop career training services to international students in the UK. We lead the international careers training sector in key performance indicators such as market share, internship placement, and customer satisfaction.

**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

1. **What do you do?** Z+ is an international student career business consultant. We help international students in the UK with career or business advancement by offering training and guidance to build their confidence and skills for securing interviews and relevant careers. We also assist early-stage entrepreneurs.  
2. **When should we meet with you?** We can help at any stage of study, but the best time to contact us is in the first year of university. Early engagement allows us to plan internships and career paths effectively.  
3. **Can you guarantee that I will get an internship I like?** No one can ethically guarantee a particular internship, but based on our track record, we are confident in promising either an internship or a refund. Success depends on clients making good progress, being prepared to accept job opportunities, and developing the necessary skills.

\-----**Services**

1. **Career Coaching:** Programmes designed to help graduates and students secure jobs in international companies. The services are structured, tailor-made, and offered one-on-one.  
2. **Start-up Networks:** We provide students access to a large network of UK start-up and SME partners. Clients receive news and updates about internship programmes and advice on which opportunities best match their goals.  
3. **Business Consulting:** We offer information, understanding, and skills for international graduates and overseas entrepreneurs who wish to start a business in the UK. This support aims to help clients grow a successful business.
